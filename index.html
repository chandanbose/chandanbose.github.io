Welcome to my website!

I am currently a Wallonie Bruxelles International Excellence Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Fluid-Structure Interactions and Experimental Aerodynamics Laboratory, Dept.of Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering, University of Liege, Belgium. I have obtained M.S. and Ph.D. from the Dept. of Applied Mechanics, IIT Madras. I am passionately interested in fluid dynamics problems of different tastes especially which are having a flavour of nonlinear dynamics. My current research focus is on understanding low speed unsteady aerodynamics/ hydrodynamics and nonlinear fluid-structure interaction behaviour of bio-inspired propulsion.
