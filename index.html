I am currently a post-doctoral research associate in the Fluid-Structure Interactions and Experimental Aerodynamics Laboratory, Dept. of Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering, University of Liège, Belgium. I also closely collaborate with the Multi-physics and Turbulent Flow Computation research group here. I have obtained my M.S. and Ph.D. degrees from the Dept. of Applied Mechanics, IIT Madras, India, where I worked at the Uncertainty Laboratory and the Biomimetics Laboratory. I am passionately interested in fluid dynamics problems of different tastes, especially with a flavour of nonlinear dynamics. My current research focus is on understanding the unsteady aerodynamics/ hydrodynamics and nonlinear fluid-structure interaction behaviour of bio-inspired propulsion.

My University of Liège profile is here.

Research Interests:
Biological Fluid Dynamics, Unsteady Aerodynamics, Fluid-Structure Interactions, Flow Induced Energy Harvesting, Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos.
